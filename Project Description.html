Project Description

In the past few years, virtual reality technology has brought us large amount of display and input devices. VR is used to create three-dimensional environments that can replace the real world. Imagine you are sitting on a chair and looking down. You see your body, from your arms to your lap, and your legs. But it is not your body, it is a virtual body created by Mel Slater, a professor of virtual environment at the University of Barcelona, is inside his lab and it is what VR are capable of. A virtual reality system consisting of hardware and software. System software includes virtual reality software programmed in various program languages to model and simulate objects. Hardware are Input devices which enhance the ability to stimulate users' senses to simulate virtual reality, such as stereophonic headsets, G-sensor location sensors, Bluetooth controls,... and output devices include display screen, impulse,... The salient feature of VR is to create virtual things, virtual environments. Thereby, it help to recreate the object that are available, or display the things that are expected to produce. Then, analyzing and making decisions will be more intuitive, more accurate, and save a lot of time. Having a virtual environment also means that people can meet, work and exchange directly anywhere in the world without actually get there. The creation of a virtual environment also supports easy remote control, creating more possibilities for production. When more possibilities can be reached, it is obvious that the opportunity for further development will be endless. By creating a deeper and more personal connection with the player, VR has a great potential of attracting gamer around the world, and has become what they are dream of these days.



Even though possibilities are endless, VR is still relatively new, so are the products is still yet to be prefect. In fact, virtual reality existed before the 2010s, but starting in 2020 was a period of tremendous growth. Reported from Fobes, it's predicted that location-based entertainment will contain approximately 11 per cent of the VR industry by 2023. location-based entertainment(LBE) is a term used for a form of entertainment such as a game in which the gameplay evolves and progresses via user's location. Therefore, these activity will provide user with some localization technology that can report their location such as VR headset. The VOID is well known in this market with various franchise it's linked to such as Star Wars, Wreck It Ralph, Ghostbusters and Avenger and with 16 opened location around the world. One of the VOID feature is that it combine VR and real-world sets, allow people to interact with real environment and object.

In this era of ever-growing technology, with the appear of AR and VR, developers look forward to giving users more realistic experiences in the virtual world by combining them in the future. Michael Abrash, the Oculus chief scientists who made this prediction states the challenges of reconstructing the real world, and states that augmented VR will be an integral part of virtual reality. Virtual reality glasses are now relatively large in size and have quite a lot of details while different brands try to make them look more polished each year. As technology becomes more advanced, devices are known to become more compact and more delicacy in design. The same thing happens with VR headsets, by 2025 they might be the style of sunglasses. This requires a huge reduction in the size of these virtual reality glasses and as a result will allow the user to use technology smoothly, especially for those who are not comfortable wearing bulky virtual reality glasses.
